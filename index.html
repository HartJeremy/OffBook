OffBook Help

OffBook is a cue-based rehearsal app. Load a script, select the role or roles you are learning, then practice from cues until you are off book.

Script format

TITLE: My Script
AUTHOR: Optional

ALICE
Where is Bob?

BOB
Right here.

ALICE
Good. [pause:2]
[block: Cross to DSR]
[note: Quiet, controlled]
[prop: keys in left hand]
Let's start.

Character names go on their own line. Dialogue goes below. Blank lines are fine.

Line tags

[pause:2]
Adds a 2 second pause. It is not read aloud and you do not have to say it.
[block: Move DSR]
Adds blocking to the current line. You can show or hide it during rehearsal.
[note: less angry]
Adds an acting note for the current line.
[prop: golf wedge]
Adds a prop reminder.

Cue modes

Last cue only gives the line immediately before yours. Last 2 cues gives two lead-in lines. Full lead-in gives all lines since your previous line.

Practice modes

In order is best for first memorization. Random tests recall. Random no repeats gives every selected line once before reshuffling. Use Needs Work, Failed, or Bookmarks to drill problem spots.

Audio feedback

Use Enable sound cues once after opening OffBook. A ding means pass. A low err tone means fail. Browser audio usually has to be enabled by a tap before it can play automatically.

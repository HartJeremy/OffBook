TITLE: Sample Script
AUTHOR: Line Trainer Example

EXT. PARK - DAY

A quiet park. Sam enters with a coffee.

SAM
I thought you said this place would be empty.

ALEX
It usually is.

SAM
Then why is there a marching band behind that tree?

ALEX
(Beat)
Because you said you wanted a dramatic entrance.

SAM
I meant for me.

ALEX
Then start marching.
